I am a second year Software Engineering student at the Center of Information Technology and Scientific Computing in Addis Ababa Institute of Technology. I have always been interested by how computers work since I was a kid. In High School, I used to learn on various aspects of computers and programming online from sites like YouTube, Khan Academy, and so on. That is why when I got the chance to study at Addis Ababa University, I joined the Software Engineering program. Since I joined I have learned a lot from the amazing staff at ITSC, who taught me to be fluent in web development, python, java language. I am also currently learning more Web development and Mobile development in java and flutter.

 

In the future I would like to focus on studying Artificial Intelligence. Especially, I would like to conduct research and work on Artificial Intelligence and its effects on developing countries like Ethiopia and also other countries in Africa.
